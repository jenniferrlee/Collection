Tiramisu Recipe

Ingredients

  • sugar
  • mascarpone cheese
  • ladyfingers
  • cocoa powder
  • instant coffee powder

Instructions

  1. Mix instant coffee powder with hot water and sugar.
  2. Whisk heavy cream with sugar until thick.
  3. Add mascarpone cheese to the cream.
  4. Dip ladyfingers in coffee mixture lightly and pan the layer.
  5. Overlay with a layer of cream mixture.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5.
  7. Top it off with cocoa powder.
